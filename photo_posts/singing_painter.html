Singing Painter

6/22/2009
I spent some time with a gentleman who paints houses for a living and recently started singing gospel music to his son for therapy. His son was born with spina bifida and had meningitis as a baby, which left him mentally handicap. Going into this assignment, I knew I did not want to only show some person with a handicap; rather, I wanted to focus on the relationship that exists between father and son.
