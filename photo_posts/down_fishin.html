Down Fishin'

6/19/2009
Recently, I have had an image in my head of a photograph by Jim Korpi showing two boys with their fishing poles standing on train tracks in Vermont. Every time I drive around looking for features, that image is all that is in my head. I have also been inspired by the work of Danny Wilcox Frazier and his project Driftess. Enough rambling. I was lucky enough to stumble across some boys carrying fishing poles, so I decided to tag along.
Sam Saccone @samccone
